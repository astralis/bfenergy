WE ARE UNITED: Students Leading on Sustainable Energy and AI

We live in an age of technological, scientific, and cultural innovation. Whether it be through nuclear fusion breakthroughs, accessible energy expansion, AI training educators in refugee camps, or blockchain increasing the security and effectiveness of aid, innovation is at the forefront of solving the world’s most pressing issues. We seek to equip ourselves and peers in our generation with the knowledge and connections necessary to tackle the world’s most pressing issues. We start at home–in our communities and in our state, while expanding our relationships nationally and globally.

Read about our diverse group, and reach out to a founder at: theodore.samuel.07@gmail.com
