Our Mission

Redefining the landscape of energy and technology by creating a more sustainable, prosperous, and competitive Texas, America, and World.

Texas is number #1 in total energy and technology. We seek to make Texas #1 in limitless sustainable energy and accessible generative AI.

Specifically, we want to take action to expand clean nuclear energy, modular nuclear energy, fusion energy, and generative artificial intelligence. We are an action-oriented, authentic group of students who believe the future is now. We think local action is needed to keep America competitive and to make the world a more safe and secure place.

Core Value 01

Our future is now.

We act with urgency because we believe the time for transformative change is this moment, not a distant tomorrow.

Core Value 02

We are action oriented.

Ideas are only as good as their execution. We prioritize hands-on projects and tangible outcomes over passive discussion.

Core Value 03

Invested in relationships.

Our greatest assets are our connections to each other and to experts. We are committed to building a powerful, collaborative network.
